# Hexoo Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 9, 2026

This Policy explains what data is processed in the **Hexoo** application, for what purposes, on what legal basis, and what rights users have. Hexoo is an individually developed social project provided as a hobby and educational project, not as a professional commercial service. Even so, the operator aims to keep data processing transparent and limited to what is needed for the service to work.

## 1. Data controller

The controller of personal data is the natural person who runs the Hexoo project.

Contact for personal-data matters:
**contact@hexoo.eu**

If the project starts operating as a business or changes its legal form, the controller details will be updated in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Scope of the service

Hexoo lets users create accounts, log in, create profiles, publish posts and comments, add likes, report content, and use moderation features. Some content, especially username, profile, posts, comments, likes, and selected metadata of public activity, may be visible to other users or visitors.

## 3. Data that may be processed

Depending on how Hexoo is used, the following categories of data may be processed:

| Category | Examples of data |
| --- | --- |
| Account data | user identifier, email address, username, account role, creation date, last activity date |
| Profile | avatar, avatar metadata, public profile name, account status |
| User content | posts, comments, images, reports, report reasons, edit or hiding information |
| Interactions | likes, like count, comment count, relation between likes and an account |
| Technical and security data | IP address, request headers, event date and time, session identifiers, activity logs, abuse-detection data |
| Moderation data | automatic content analysis results, violation categories, moderation decisions, information about account bans or restrictions |
| Communication | email address and message content if you contact the operator |

Hexoo is not intended for storing special categories of data, such as health data, political opinions, religion, sexual orientation, or identity documents. Do not publish this type of information unless it is necessary.

## 4. Purposes and legal bases

Data is processed mainly for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Legal basis |
| --- | --- |
| creating and maintaining an account, login, session maintenance | Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR - performance of the service agreement |
| publishing profiles, posts, comments, and likes | Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR |
| protection against spam, bots, abuse, attacks, and unauthorized access | Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR - controller's legitimate interest |
| content moderation, handling reports, bans, and account restrictions | Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR |
| technical and diagnostic logging | Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR |
| responding to messages and reports | Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR or Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR |
| complying with legal obligations if they arise | Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR |
| optional settings, such as interface preferences | Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR or consent where required |

The controller's legitimate interests include service security, spam and abuse prevention, user protection, handling reports, enforcing the Terms, diagnosing errors, protecting against claims, and maintaining infrastructure stability.

## 5. Providing data

Providing data required to create and maintain an account, such as email address and username, is necessary to use features that require an account. If you do not provide this data, registration, login, or some Hexoo features may be unavailable.

Providing data in user-published content is voluntary, but public content may be visible to other people. Do not publish data that you do not want to disclose.

## 6. Public nature of content

Hexoo is a social service. Content published as posts, comments, avatar, username, or profile elements may be public. Do not publish information that you do not want to disclose to other people. Removing content from the active service view does not always mean immediate removal from every technical copy, such as backups, logs, or infrastructure-provider caches.

## 7. Cookies, local storage, and session

Hexoo uses cookies and similar technologies mainly to operate the service, keep it secure, and remember settings.

| Name / type | Purpose | Example period |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `session` | keeping an active user session | up to 7 days |
| `refresh` | refreshing the session without logging in again | up to 365 days or less if the token is revoked |
| local storage, such as theme settings | remembering interface preferences | until removed by the user or the application |
| cookies / Google reCAPTCHA mechanisms | protection against bots and abuse | according to Google's rules and browser configuration |

Session cookies are necessary for login and security. You can restrict cookies in your browser, but some Hexoo features may then stop working properly.

## 8. reCAPTCHA and bot protection

Hexoo may use **Google reCAPTCHA v3** in login, registration, or other forms exposed to abuse. reCAPTCHA analyzes technical and behavioral signals to assess whether a request comes from a human or a bot. In this process, data may be transferred to Google according to Google's privacy rules, terms, and reCAPTCHA documentation.

## 9. Automatic moderation and content analysis

Hexoo may use automatic moderation tools, including **OpenAI** integrations, to detect spam, harmful content, Terms violations, or content requiring manual review. A content fragment, technical metadata, or classification result needed to assess a report or publication may be transferred to the provider.

Automatic moderation can be wrong. Content may be flagged, hidden, rejected, or sent for review. If you believe a decision was incorrect, you can contact the operator at the address listed in the contact section.

## 10. External technology providers

Hexoo may use external providers. Their involvement depends on the current project configuration.

| Provider | Role in the project | Possible data categories |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Supabase** | database, authentication, storage, backend | account data, content, files, application logs, user identifiers |
| **Vercel** | hosting, CDN, server functions, infrastructure logs | IP address, request headers, technical logs, data sent through the application |
| **Cloudflare** | DNS, CDN, traffic protection, email forwarding | IP address, request headers, technical logs, email metadata and correspondence sent to the contact address |
| **Google / Gmail** | receiving correspondence forwarded from Hexoo contact addresses | sender email, subject, message content, attachments, correspondence metadata |
| **Google reCAPTCHA** | protection against bots and abuse | browser technical data, reCAPTCHA tokens, risk score |
| **OpenAI** | automatic content moderation | reported or published content, classification results, moderation metadata |
| **Resend** | system messages or emails if active | email address, system-message content, delivery metadata |

Providers may act as processors on behalf of the controller or as independent controllers for their own services, security, and accountability. They may use subprocessors and process data outside the European Economic Area. In such cases, appropriate legal mechanisms are used, such as data-processing agreements, standard contractual clauses, or other safeguards required by the GDPR.

## 11. How long data is stored

Data is stored only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, unless the law requires a longer period.

| Data | Approximate period |
| --- | --- |
| account and profile | for as long as the account exists |
| posts, comments, likes | until removed by the user or administrator, or until account deletion, subject to technical copies |
| reports and moderation logs | for the period needed for security, handling reports, and abuse prevention |
| activity and security logs | for the period needed for diagnostics, security, and claim protection |
| session cookies | according to cookie validity or until logout / cookie removal |
| correspondence | for the time needed to handle the matter and possible claim protection |

After account deletion, some data may be removed, anonymized, or retained for a limited time if necessary for security, accountability, report handling, or legal obligations.

## 12. Your rights

You have the right to ask us for access to your data, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing.

In some situations, you may also object to processing, ask for data portability, or withdraw consent if processing is based on consent.

To exercise your rights, write to: contact@hexoo.eu.

We respond to personal-data requests without undue delay, usually no later than within one month. If the matter is more complex, the response may take longer, but we will inform you.

If the request concerns a specific user account, we may ask for information needed to confirm that the request comes from the correct person.

If you believe that your data is processed unlawfully, you may lodge a complaint with the President of the Personal Data Protection Office in Poland.

## 13. Children and minors

Hexoo is not directed to children. If you are under 16, using the service may require consent or approval from a parent or legal guardian. If the operator learns that an account was created in violation of these rules, the account may be restricted or deleted.

## 14. Security

Technical and organizational security measures are used, such as HTTP-only cookies, access restrictions, data validation, anti-spam protections, and moderation mechanisms. However, no web application can be guaranteed fully secure. Hexoo is individually developed, so bugs, interruptions, or vulnerabilities may occur and will be fixed where possible.

## 15. Changes to this Policy

This Policy may be updated as Hexoo develops, technology providers change, or the law changes. The new version applies from the moment it is published in the service, unless stated otherwise.
